The Customer Support screen provides a direct messaging interface between you and the DOSS support team. Access it from **Profile → Customer Support** (`CUSTOMER_SUPPORT` route).

## The support chat interface

The screen displays a scrollable conversation thread. Messages from the support team appear on the left; your messages appear on the right. Each message shows a timestamp.

To send a message, type in the input field at the bottom of the screen and submit. The input includes a microphone icon for future voice input support.

<Note>
  The chat interface is a live messaging screen. Conversation history is loaded as part of the screen and scrolls vertically.
</Note>

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my DOSS wallet to buy alcohol?">
    No. DOSS funds cannot be used for purchases of alcoholic products. This restriction is enforced at the merchant level.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I update my personal information?">
    Go to **Profile → Preferences** to update your name, email address, or phone number. See the [Preferences](/profile/preferences) page for step-by-step instructions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I verify my identity?">
    Upload your identity documents from **Profile → Account Security**. You can submit an ID proof, passport, or driving licence. See the [Account security](/profile/account-security) page for instructions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my PIN. How do I reset it?">
    Tap **Reset PIN** on the Profile screen. You will be prompted to set and confirm a new 4-digit PIN. See [Resetting your PIN](/profile/account-security#resetting-your-pin) for the full flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I sign out of the app?">
    Tap **Logout** at the bottom of the Profile screen and confirm the prompt. See the [Profile overview](/profile/overview#signing-out) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for faster support

<Tip>
  Include your User ID (shown on the Profile screen) when describing an issue. This helps the support team locate your account quickly.
</Tip>

- Describe the issue clearly, including the screen you were on and what action you were taking.
- If the issue involves a transaction, include the date, amount, and merchant name.
- For document upload issues, confirm which document type you were trying to submit.
