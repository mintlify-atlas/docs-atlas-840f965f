The Preferences screen lets you update your name, email address, and phone number. It also provides access to your group memberships and document re-verification.

Navigate here from **Profile → Preferences** (`PREFERENCES` route).

## Available options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Update name" icon="user" href="#update-name">
    Change your first name and last name.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Update email" icon="envelope" href="#update-email">
    Replace your current email address with a new one.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Update phone number" icon="phone" href="#update-phone-number">
    Change your registered phone number.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Groups" icon="users" href="/features/groups">
    View your group memberships and join or leave groups.
  </Card>

<Note>
  All update forms use `react-hook-form` with `zod` schema validation. Validation runs client-side before any API call is made.
</Note>

## Update name

**Route:** `UPDATE_NAME`

Change the first name and last name on your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Update Name">
    Tap **Update Name** on the Preferences screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your names">
    Fill in both fields:

    - **First Name** — required, auto-capitalised.
    - **Last Name** — required, auto-capitalised.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **Update**. The app calls:

    ```http
    PUT /v2/update_name
    ```

    ```json Request body
    {
      "first_name": "Jane",
      "last_name": "Smith"
    }
    ```

    On success, the profile cache is invalidated and you are returned to the Preferences screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Update email

**Route:** `UPDATE_EMAIL`

Replace your registered email address.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Update Email">
    Tap **Update Email** on the Preferences screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email addresses">
    Fill in both fields:

    - **Old Email** — your current email address. Must be a valid email format.
    - **New Email Address** — the email you want to switch to. Must be a valid email format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **Update**. The app calls:

    ```http
    PUT /v2/update_email
    ```

    ```json Request body
    {
      "last_email": "old@example.com",
      "new_email": "new@example.com"
    }
    ```

    On success, the profile cache is invalidated and you are returned to the Preferences screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You must supply your current email address correctly. If the old email does not match your account, the request will fail.
</Warning>

## Update phone number

**Route:** `UPDATE_NUMBER`

Change the phone number associated with your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Update Phone Number">
    Tap **Update Phone Number** on the Preferences screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your current number">
    The input field is pre-populated with the country dial code (defaulting to `+1868` for Trinidad and Tobago). Tap the flag icon to open the country picker and select a different country if needed.

    Type your current phone number after the prefix. The prefix is immutable — if you delete it, it is restored automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new number">
    Repeat the same process for your new phone number, selecting the correct country code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **Next**. The app calls:

    ```http
    PUT /v2/update_phone
    ```

    ```json Request body
    {
      "old_phone": "+18681234567",
      "new_phone": "+18687654321"
    }
    ```

    On success, the profile cache is invalidated and you are returned to the Preferences screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Both fields require a non-empty phone number. Submitting with either field blank shows an "Invalid Number" error and no API call is made.
</Note>
